Introduction

Who is PERLUR?

PERLUR started up in 2010 back when Public Cloud was just a buzzword, and a thing used by startups who could not afford capital expenditure associated with buying hardware, managed IT services were used instead by larger enterprises. At that time, our founder and Managing Partner -- Mark Stopka was moving to Finland to take on a more involved role at the new emerging service line within Tieto (now TietoEVRY) prividing NAS Data Storage capacity from NetApp FAS Unified Data Storage arrays inside Tieto datacenters to it's enterprise customers.
