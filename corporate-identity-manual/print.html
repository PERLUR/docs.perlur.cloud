Introduction

Who is PERLUR?

PERLUR started up in 2010 back when Public Cloud was just a buzzword, and a thing used by startups who could not afford capital expenditure associated with buying hardware, managed IT services were used instead by larger enterprises. At that time, our founder and Managing Partner -- Mark Stopka was moving to Finland to take on a more involved role at the new emerging service line within Tieto (now TietoEVRY) prividing NAS Data Storage capacity from NetApp FAS Unified Data Storage arrays inside Tieto datacenters to it's enterprise customers.

Our purpose

PERLUR Group revolutionizes the world by being the international investment firm that acquires significant ownership in publically listed and privately owned companies where long-term value can be improved thru synergies and active management across our Private Equity portfolio as well as thru our Venture Capital activities. In addition, we also provide Business and Technology Consulting services globally.

We enable companies to start, grow, increase market share and expand to whole new markets, while leveraging cutting edge technology that enables equal access to economic opportunities globally.

Overview

Meet our basic PERLUR Group logo, it is the most important thing about out brand.

The PERLUR logo is clean, with confident mark made up of geometric forms. It has two core elements, the graphic and the PERLUR wordmark. Together they create the PERLUR Group logo. In addition to this basic logo with dual color logomark, we also have logo with monochromatic logomark.
